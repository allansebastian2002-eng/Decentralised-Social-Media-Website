# PROJECTNAME  
Decentralised-Social-Media-Website
## Objective
- To create a free to use decentralized blockchain based social media website.
- Create an advertisement free environment for the users 
- All the data of the users will be protected and cannot be sold to third party sites.
- To create a decentralized social media platform.
- The website will help people connect with each other and share their thoughts without compromising on their privacy or having to deal with advertisements.


### Existing system limitations
- It stores data in a single physical datacenter. 
- Data can be manipulated .
- Major privacy concerns.
- Data can be sold for advertisement purposes. 
- A single company can create their monopoly.

### Proposed System Advantages

-  Secure and protects privacy of the user.
- No risk of data leakage. 
- Secure payment through social media. 
- its Reliable.
- Lesser to no involvement of government or any kind of companies

## Resource requirements
- Solidity
- Ethereum Blockchain
- Thirdweb API 
- BNB Smart Chain Testnet

  ## Result and Analysis
  
- The authenticaton occurs securely and fast.
- All the status updates made are passed as transactions through blockchain. 
- All status transactions can be seen in the BNB testnet page.
- Once uploaded the transactions can not be erased by anyone other than the owner.

## Advantages
- It has a faster and more secure way of logging in as the system does not rely on email verification and uses crypto wallet to log in the user.
- All the status updates made are recorded as transactions and can be verified in the account’s transaction history in the testnet page.
- No advertisements.
- No risk of data leakage or manipulation.

## Future Scope
- Web 3.0 is the way to go for the future as the existing web2.0 system is getting outdated.
- In coming years social media websites will be ad free and more secure.
- Payments and purchases through social media websites will become more secure.
- More large scale projects can come true
